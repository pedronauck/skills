# YC Application Form — Summer 2026 batch

Bundled default form spec. Phase 0 copies this to `<workspace>/01_form-spec.md` unless the founder pastes a fresher live version from apply.ycombinator.com. When YC changes the form, add a new `form-spec-<batch>.md` rather than editing this file.

> Field-stakes legend: **[HIGH]** = runs the full grill loop in Phase 4; **[LOW]** = prompt + quick validation only.

## Team & Roles

- **[HIGH]** Who writes code, or does other technical work on your product? Was any of it done by a non-founder? Please explain.
- **[LOW]** Are you looking for a cofounder? (yes/no)

## Founder Video

- **[HIGH]** Required: a one-minute video introducing the founder(s). File ≤ 100 MB. (Phase 5 produces bullet notes.)

## Company

- **[LOW]** Company name (required).
- **[HIGH]** Describe what your company does in 50 characters or less (required).
- **[LOW]** Company URL (if any).

## Demo

- **[LOW]** Optional demo upload (≤ 3 minutes / 100 MB).
- **[LOW]** Product URL.
- **[LOW]** Login credentials for the product URL (username / password).

## Product Description

- **[HIGH]** What is your company going to make? Describe your product and what it does or will do.

## Location

- **[LOW]** Where do you live now, and where would the company be based after YC? Format: `City A, Country A / City B, Country B`.
- **[LOW]** Explain your decision regarding location.

## Progress

- **[HIGH]** How far along are you?
- **[LOW]** How long have each of you been working on this? How much of that has been full-time? Please explain.
- **[LOW]** What tech stack are you using, or planning to use, to build this product? Include AI models and AI coding tools you use.
- **[HIGH·EXPERIMENTAL]** Optional (Summer 2026 experimental): attach a coding-agent session you're particularly proud of. Many coding agents have a `/export` command. `.md` or `.txt`, ≤ 25 MB. (Phase 4 coding-agent sub-step.)

## Traction

- **[LOW]** Are people using your product? (yes/no)
- **[LOW]** Do you have revenue? (yes/no)
- **[HIGH·REAPPLICANT]** If you are applying with the same idea as a previous batch, did anything change? If you applied with a different idea, why did you pivot and what did you learn from the last idea?
- **[LOW]** If you have already participated in or committed to an incubator/accelerator/pre-accelerator, tell us about it.

## Idea

- **[HIGH]** Why did you pick this idea to work on? Do you have domain expertise in this area? How do you know people need what you're making?
- **[HIGH]** Who are your competitors? What do you understand about your business that they don't?
- **[HIGH]** How do or will you make money? How much could you make? (Best estimate.)
- **[HIGH]** If you had any other ideas you considered applying with, please list them. One may be something we've been waiting for.

## Equity

- **[LOW]** Have you formed ANY legal entity yet? (yes/no)
- **[LOW]** Have you taken any investment yet? (yes/no)
- **[LOW]** Are you currently fundraising? (yes/no)

## Curious

- **[LOW]** What convinced you to apply to Y Combinator? Did someone encourage you to apply? Have you been to any YC events?
- **[LOW]** How did you hear about Y Combinator?

## Batch Preference

- **[LOW]** Batch selection. (For Early Decision, select a batch after the current one and note the deferral reason.)

---

## Authoring notes

- The coding-agent session field is NEW for Summer 2026 and experimental. Treat it as a signal field, not a throwaway attachment.
- The 50-char company description is a hard constraint; draft ≥5 candidates and grill to the best.
- The tech-stack field now explicitly asks for AI models + AI coding tools — probe for both.
- The location field now has a post-YC component; founders abroad must address relocation/remote intent.
